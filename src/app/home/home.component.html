Welcome to the public APIs project

Here you can try out various APIs with an angular app that's built by me.
Each page interacts with a different set of APIs, all of which are publicly available on the internet. No authentication required.

Choose one of the pages from the menu above and see what they are about.

Note: The CoinGecko page only displays data, but the cryptocurrency data is fetched from coingecko.com each time you load the page.

The third page (TvMaze) is the most complex one, and it includes searching a tv show database, saving specific tv shows in the localstorage, and deleting if you want to free up storage space for other shows.
